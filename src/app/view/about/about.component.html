For my entire life up to now I have watched others perform magic, they were few, but fantastical and filled my heart with wonder.
Practice and study as you may, I was never able to recreate even simple cantrips that the local wizard tried to teach me. Distraught, I ran to the woods to a secret clearing to cry for a bit. That day something incredible happened, magic shot from my fingertips, and shortly after a man in a suit and tie walked up to me from the edge of the clearing and sat his briefcase down on a large flat rock that I had never seen sitting there before. “It seems we have some paperwork to go over,” he said with a smile and my life changed forever.
